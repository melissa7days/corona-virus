What is Influenza?

Influenza is a viral infection that attacks your respiratory system — your nose, throat and lungs. Influenza is commonly called the flu, but it's not the same as stomach "flu" viruses.

What is Covid-19?

A Coronavirus is a kind of common virus that causes an infection in your nose, sinuses, or upper throat. They’re called coronaviruses because under a microscope, they look like a crown.

Covid-19 Symptoms

People may be sick with the virus for 1 to 14 days before developing symptoms. The most common symptoms of coronavirus disease (COVID-19) are fever, tiredness, and dry cough. Most people (about 80%) recover from the disease without needing special treatment.

People may experience:

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Difficulty Breathing (severe cases)
